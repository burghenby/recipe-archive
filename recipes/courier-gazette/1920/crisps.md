## Directions - Original

Cream one cupful of butter, add gradually two cupfuls of sugar, three eggs beaten until light, the grated rind and juice of a lemon and flour to make a mixture to roll. Kneed slightly, cut in shapes, brush with white of an egg, sprinkle with coarse granulated sugar and chopped nuts. Bake a light brown.
