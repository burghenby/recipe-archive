# Chicken Parmesan

- **Source**: *[Bravado Spice](https://bravadospice.com/)* Chicken Parmesan recipe card
  (Note: This recipe is [available on Bravado Spice's website](https://bravadospice.com/blogs/recipes/chicken-parmesan), though there are some slight differences to the recipe card below.)
---
**Total Time**: 3.5 hours\
**Makes**: 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 4 Large chicken breasts
- 1.5 oz Bravado Spice Serrano & Basil Hot sauce ([Still listed available](https://bravadospice.com/products/serrano-basil-hot-sauce?_pos=1&_sid=89f073fb4&_ss=r) as of 1-20-26)
- 3 oz. Fresh mozzarella, sliced
- 2 c. All-purpose flour
- 3 Eggs, beaten
- 2 c. Bread crumbs
- 1/4 c. Parmesan cheese
- 2 c. Oil for frying
- Fresh parsley for garnish
- Cooked pasta in sauce

## Directions

1. Butterfly the chicken breasts by partially slicing them lengthwise towards the thickest part. Then open them u; on a cutting board. Pour oil in a pot large enough to handle them and heat to 350°F.
2. Put some plastic wrap over the breast and gently flatten them with a meat tenderizer. Sprinkle each side with about a teaspoon of salt.
3. Take each breast and dredge it in flour, then the egg wash, then the bread crumbs, then gently lay in the hot oil with some tongs.
4. Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown then cool on a rack.
5. Pour the Bravado Spice Serrano & Basil Hot Sauce on top of the breasts, and then lay the sliced mozzarella medallions on top.
6. Broil at 500°F on the baking rack until the cheese is browned & bubbly then plate the chicken on some pasta with sauce, garnished with parsley and fresh parmesan.
