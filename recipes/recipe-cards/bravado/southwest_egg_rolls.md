# Southwest Egg Rolls

- **Source**: *[Bravado Spice](https://bravadospice.com/)* Southwest Egg Rolls recipe card
  (Note: Bravado Spice's website has a recipe section, however I could not locate this recipe among their offerings.)
---
**Total Time**: 30 min\
**Makes**: 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 oz. Bravado Spice Crimson Hot Sauce (See note below)
- 2 c. Chicken, shredded
- 1 c. Black beans, cooked
- 1 c. Frozen spinach, thawed
- 2/3 c. Canned corn
- 2/3 c. Red onion, chopped
- 2/3 c. Red pepper, chopped
- 1 c. Parmesan, shredded
- 1 tsp. each Salt & pepper
- 1 tsp. Cumin
- 1 tsp. Chili powder
- 20 Flour tortillas
- Oil for frying

**Note**: Bravado Spice Crimson Hot Sauce is no longer listed. The closest equivalent based on Crimson's ingredients seems to be their [Árbol Chili & Garlic Hot Sauce](https://bravadospice.com/products/arbol-chili-garlic-hot-sauce).\
**_Crimson Hot Sauce_**: White Vinegar, Red Jalapeños, Arbol Chilies, Garlic, Sea Salt, Sugar ([Source](https://crafthotsauce.com/products/bravado-spice-co-crimson-hot-sauce))\
**_Árbol Chili & Garlic Hot Sauce_**: Distilled Vinegar, Jalapeño Pepper Puree, Chile De Arbol Pepper, Garlic, Lime Juice, Cumin, Black Pepper, Salt

## Directions

1. Mix the chicken, beans, cheese, red onion, red pepper, corn, spinach, cumin, chili powder, salt & pepper, and Bravado Spice Crimson Hot Sauce into a large mixing bowl and stir until completely mixed.
2. Heat your oil to 325°. Separately mix 3 tablespoons of water in flour in a small bowl to make a slurry.
3. While the oil heats, take two T of the chicken  filling and spoon it into the middle of a flour tortilla. Then roll one side about 1/4 of the way up. Tuck both sides and roll until almost completely rolls. Then take a little of the flour slurry and spread it on the last little bit of the tortilla. Use it to seal the roll. Repeat till all the tortillas or filling is used.
4. Carefully place the rolls in the hot oil and fry until golden brown. Serve with your favorite dipping sauce.
