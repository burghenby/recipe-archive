# Prime Rib

- **Source**: *[Bravado Spice](https://bravadospice.com/)* Prime Rib recipe card
  (Note: Bravado Spice's website has a recipe section, however I could not locate this recipe among their offerings.)
---
**Total Time**: 85 min\
**Makes**: 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 tsp. Minced garlic
- 3 T. Bravado Spice Black Garlic & Carolina Reaper Hot sauce ([Still listed available](https://bravadospice.com/products/black-garlic-carolina-reaper-hot-sauce?_pos=1&_sid=07f138843&_ss=r) as of 1-20-26)
- 1 T. Salted butter, softened
- 1 T. Cracked black pepper
- 1 T. Thyme, minced
- 3 lb. Prime rib, bone in, frenched

## Directions

1. Mixed the softened butter, salt, pepper, garlic, and Bravado Spice Black Garlic & Carolina Reaper Hot Sauce in a bowl until completely incorporated. Preheat your oven to 250°F.
2. Slather the prime rib with the butter on all sides.
3. Set the prime rib on a rack with a drain and bake for one hour at 250°F. Then raise the temperature to 500°F and back for an additional 10 minutes.
4. Remove the prime rib from the oven and let set for 10 minutes. Slice and serve.
