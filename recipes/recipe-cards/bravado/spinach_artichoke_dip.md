# Spinach Artichoke Dip

- **Source**: *[Bravado Spice](https://bravadospice.com/)* Spinach Artichoke Dip recipe card
  (Note: Bravado Spice's recipe section of their website has a recipe for "Spinach Artichoke Dip." However, the recipe is now different and utilizes a different hot sauce from the recipe card detailed below.)
---
**Total Time**: 30 min\
**Makes**: 1 serving

## Ingredients

- 1 c. Frozen spinach, thawed and drained
- 1-1/2 c. Frozen artichoke hearts, thawed and drained
- 2-1/2 oz. Bravado Spice Jalapeño & Green Apple Hot Sauce (See note below)
- 8 oz. Cream cheese, warmed
- 6 oz. Sour cream
- 1/3 c. Monterrey jack
- 1/3 c. Shredded parmesan
- 1/2 tsp. Minced garlic
- 1/2 tsp. each Salt & Pepper
- Chips for dipping

(Note as of 1-20-26: Bravado Spice lists the [Jalapeño & Green Apple Hot Sauce](https://bravadospice.com/products/jalapeno-green-apple-hot-sauce?_pos=1&_sid=3632855ad&_ss=r) as a Limited Flavor)

## Directions

1. Preheat an oven to 375°F.
2. Try to remove as much liquid as possible from the thawed spinach and artichokes.
3. Combine the spinach, artichokes, cream cheese, sour cream, garlic, salt, pepper, parmesan, and Monterrey jack cheese, and Bravado Spice Jalapeño & Green Apple Hot Sauce in a large bowl and mix.
4. Pour the mixture into a casserole dish or, similar oven safe dish, and bake for 20-25 minutes or until slightly browned and bubbly.
5. Serve immediately with chips. To keep warm for the duration of a party, cook in a slow cooker instead of the oven.
