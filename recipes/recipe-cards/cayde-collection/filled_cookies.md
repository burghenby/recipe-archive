# Filled Cookies (raisins, cherries, dates etc.)

**Source**: Grandma Ada Hargest
---

## Ingredients

- 1 c. Shortening
- 2 c. Sugar
- 3 Eggs
- 6 c. Flour
- 3 tsp. Baking Powder (heaping)
- 1 tsp. Salt
- 1 tsp. Vanilla

## Directions

Beat together shortening and sugar till light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla and beat well. In a separate bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, and salt. Combine with wet ingredients till blended./
Roll dough out onto lightly floured surface making sure dough is rolled thin. Cut out circles using cookie cutter or drinking glass with lightly floured edges to prevent sticking and tearing the dough./
Drop filling by teaspoonful into center of every other circle of dough. Place other dough circle on top of filling and crimp edges. Prick tops of cookies to vent steam./
Bake for 10-15 mins at 375°F.
