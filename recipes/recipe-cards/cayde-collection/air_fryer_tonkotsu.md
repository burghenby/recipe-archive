# Air Fryer Tonkotsu

**Source**: Personal
---

## Ingredients

- Pork (steaks, loin, cutlets, etc)
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 2 Large Eggs
- Bread or Panko
- Non-stick Cooking Spray

## Directions

Dredge pork twice.
Air fry at 350°F for 10 minutes, flip, spray, 10 more min.
