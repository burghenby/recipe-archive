# Coca Fudge

**Source**: Grandma Ada Hargest
---

## Ingredients

- 4 c. Sugar
- 2/3 c. Cocoa
- 1-1/2 c. Milk
- 2 tsp. Light Corn Syrup
- 1/4 tsp. Salt
- 6 T. Butter
- 2 tsp. Vanilla

## Directions

Measure sugar, cocoa, milk, corn syrup and salt into heavy bottomed 3 quart saucepan. mix well. Cook over medium heat and stir until it begins to boil. Boil until it reaches soft ball stages, stirring occasionally./
Remove from heat and add butter and vanilla. Let cool without stirring unti lukewarm. Beat until thick. Pour into greased or buttered pan and refrigerate.
