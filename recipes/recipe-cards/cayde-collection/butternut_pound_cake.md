# Butternut Pound Cake

**Source**: From Jean's Kitchen
---

## Ingredients

- 3 c. Sugar
- 1/2 c. Shortening
- 2 sticks Butter
- 1/4 tsp. Salt
- 5 large Eggs
- 1 c. Walnuts, chopped
- 3 c. Flour
- 1 small can Evaporated Milk + enough water to make 1 cup (Get specific can size)
- 2 T. Butternut flavoring
- 1-10 oz. jar Maraschino cherries, rinsed and cut in pieces

## Directions

Cream sugar, shortening, butter and salt together. Add eggs, one at a time. Add flour, alternating with milk. Add flavoring, cherries and walnuts./
Grease and flour tube pan or angel food cake pan. [Pour batter into pan.]/
START in COLD oven. Bake at 325°F for 2-2 1/2 hours. DO NOT open oven door during baking.
