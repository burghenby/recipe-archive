# Potato Salad Dressing

**Source**: Grandma Ada Hargest
---

## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs, beaten
- 2 T. Cornstarch
- 3/4 c. Milk
- 4 T. Sugar
- 1/4 c. Butter
- 1/2 tsp. Salt
- 1/2 tsp. Pepper
- Vinegar to taste

## Directions

Combine eggs, cornstarch, salt, milk, and sugar in heavy saucepan. Cook over medium heat till mixture thickens and becomes lumpy and begins to get dry looking./
Stir constantly to prevent scorching. Remove from heat and add butter and vinegar. Stir until smooth as possible./
Pour over potatoes while it is still warm as it helps flavor it more.
