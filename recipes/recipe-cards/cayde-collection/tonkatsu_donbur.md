# Tonkatsu Donbur

**Source**: Personal
---

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup Chicken Broth
- 2 tsp. White Sugar
- 2 tsp. Mirin (Japanese Sweet Wine)
- 2 tsp. Soy Sauce
- 1/2 tsp. Salt
- 1 T. Oil/Butter
- 1 Small onion, chopped (half onion)
- 2 Eggs, beaten
- Rice

## Directions

Mix broth, sugar, mirin, soy sauce & salt./
Heat oil, cook onion 5 min. Add broth mix./
Lay slices (?) on top and drizzle egg around in skillet. Cover for 2 min.
