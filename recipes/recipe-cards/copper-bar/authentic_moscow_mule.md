# Authentic Moscow Mule

- **Source**: *[Copper Bar](https://www.instagram.com/copper_bar_official/)* Moscow Mule Mugs Recipe Book
---
**Makes**: 1 cocktail

## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Vodka
- 1 oz. Lime juice
- 1/2 c. Ginger beer
- 1 chunk Candied ginger
- 12 Raspberries
- 6 Mint leaves, for taste and a sprig for garnish
- 4 slices Cucumber

## Directions

1. Take the cucumber slice, mint leaves and 8 berries out of the raspberry bunch and put them inside a cocktail shaker at the bottom. Muddle the three until they are juicy and crushed fully. Now pour vodka, lime juice and a fistful of ice. Shake the cocktail shaker wwith all the ingredients for another 15 seconds until you can see condensation from the ice on the outer cover of the shaker.
2. Take a Moscow mule mug and fill it with ice. Pour the liquid into the mug and top it with berries as well as ginger beer. You can add additional garnish with remaining berries along with ginger chunk.
