# Summer Refreshers

- **Source**: *[Copper Bar](https://www.instagram.com/copper_bar_official/)* Moscow Mule Mugs Recipe Book
---
**Makes**: 1 cocktail

## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Vodka
- 4-6 oz. Ginger beer, cold
- Juice from half wedge of lime
- 1 wheel Lime, for garnish

## Directions

Take a highball glass or the Moscow Mule filled with ice and pour vodka. Now fill in the cold ginger beer and top the glass with lime wheel for garnishing.
