# Maui Style Moscow Mule

- **Source**: *[Copper Bar](https://www.instagram.com/copper_bar_official/)* Moscow Mule Mugs Recipe Book
---
**Makes**: 1 cocktail

## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Vodka; You can also choose potato-based vodka it you want to be gluten-free
- 1 c. Ice
- 1/2 oz. Pimm's No. 1
- 1/2 oz. Lime juice
- 4 oz. Ginger beer
- 1 sprig Mint (optional)
- 1 chunk Candied ginger (optional)
- 1 lime [Note: Recipe lists "Lemon Juice, 1 lime" but method makes no mention of lemon, so I believe this is a typo.]

## Directions

Get your favorite copper cup and fill it with ice. Add vodka, lime juice and Pimm's to the mug. Pour ginger beer on to the mix and stir lightly. Add garnish of mint, lime squeeze and ginger chunk for added flavor and aroma.
