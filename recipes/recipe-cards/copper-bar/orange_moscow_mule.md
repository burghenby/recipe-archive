# Orange Moscow Mule

- **Source**: *[Copper Bar](https://www.instagram.com/copper_bar_official/)* Moscow Mule Mugs Recipe Book
---
**Makes**: 1 cocktail

## Ingredients

- 2 oz. Orange juice, freshly squeezed
- 1 oz. Lemon juice, freshly squeezed
- 1 c. ice
- 2 oz. Vodka
- 1/2 c. Ginger beer
- 1 piece Candied ginger, for garnish
- 1 slice Orange or tangerine, for garnish
- 1 sprig Mint, for garnish

## Directions

Grab your Moscow mule mug and fill it with ice. Pour orange juice, ginger beer and vodka. Stir lightly and to top it with slice of orange, mint sprig and ginger.
