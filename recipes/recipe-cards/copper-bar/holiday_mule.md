# Holiday Mule

- **Source**: *[Copper Bar](https://www.instagram.com/copper_bar_official/)* Moscow Mule Mugs Recipe Book
---
**Makes**: 1 cocktail

## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz. Flavored syrup
- 1/2 oz. Cranberry juice, unsweetened
- 1-1/2 oz. Vodka or gin
- 2 oz. Ginger beer, chilled
- 1 sprig Rosemary
- 1 slice Orange, for garnish
- Sweetened cranberries, for garnish

## Directions

Mix the ingredients of cranberry juice, gin and simple syrup. Pour this mix in a Moscow mule filled with Ice. Top it off with ginger beer. Garnish the drink with rosemary, wedge of orange and sweetened cranberries for added taste.
