# Mini Chocolate Raspberry Cakes

**Source**: *Taste of Home* magazine, probably early-mid 2000's; Submitted by Pamela Shank
---
**Makes**: 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 Eggs
- 1 c. Sugar
- 1/2 c. Water
- 1/2 c. Canola Oil
- 1/2 tsp. Raspberry Extract
- 1/4 tsp. Vanilla Extract
- 1 c. All-purpose Flour
- 1/2 c. Baking Cocoa
- 1-1/2 tsp. Baking Soda
- 1/4 tsp. Salt

**Raspberry Sauce**
-1/4 c. Sugar
1 T. Cornstarch
2 c. Fresh Raspberries
1/2 c. Water

**Topping**
- 1 c. Vanilla Ice Cream
- 1 c. Hot Fudge Ice Cream Topping, warmed
- Chopped pecans and additional fresh raspberries, optional

## Directions

- In a bowl, beat eggs, sugar, water, oil and extracts until well blended. In another bowl, combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt. Gradually beat into sugar mixture until blended.
- Pour into four 4-inch fluted tube pans or  6-oz. ramekins coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350°F for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pans to a wire rack to cool completely.
- For raspberry sauce, in a saucepan, combine sugar and cornstarch. Stir in raspberries and water; bring to a boil. Cook and stir for 1-2 minutes or until thickened. Remove from the heat; cool slightly. Press through a sieve; discard seeds.
- Scoop ice cream into cake centers. Drizzle with ice cream topping and raspberry sauce; sprinkle with pecans and additional raspberries if desired.
