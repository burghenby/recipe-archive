# Peanut Butter Cheesecake Pretzel Dip

- **Source**: 
---

## Ingredients

- 8 oz. Cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 c. Sour cream
- 1/2 c. Powdered sugar
- 1/2 c. Peanut butter
- 1/2 tsp. Vanilla

## Directions

Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy. Beat in sour cream and powdered sugar until incorporated. Beat in peanut butter and vanilla. Serve with pretzels.
