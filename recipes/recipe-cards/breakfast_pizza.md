# Breakfast Pizza

**Source**: *Produce for Kids* recipe card available at Meijer grocery stores in 2018
---
**Makes**: 2 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 (6-inch) WHole Wheat Pita
- 3 T. Almond Butter
- 1 Lil Snappers Gala Apple, sliced
- 1 T. Sliced Almonds
- 1 tsp. Honey

## Directions

Toast pita. Top with almond butter, apples and almonds. Drizzle with honey and cut into quarters.
