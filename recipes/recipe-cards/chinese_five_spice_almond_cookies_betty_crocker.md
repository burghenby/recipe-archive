# Chinese Five-Spice Almond Cookies

**Source**: *Betty Crocker* grocery store recipe card, circe 2013
---

## Ingredients

- 1 pouch Betty Crocker sugar cookie mix
- 2 tsp. Chinese Five-Spice Powder
- 1/2 c. Butter or Margarine, softened
- 1 Egg
- 1/2 c. Sliced Almonds
- 3 T. Coarse White Sparkling Sugar

## Directions

1. Heat oven to 350°F. In large bowl, stir cookie mix and five-spice powder until blended. Stir in butter and egg until soft dough forms. Stir in almonds.
2. Roll dough into 36 (1-inch) balls. Dip one side of each ball into coarse sugar. On ungreased cookie sheets, place balls, sugar side up, 2 inches apart.
3. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until edges just begin to brown. Cool 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheets to cooling racks.
