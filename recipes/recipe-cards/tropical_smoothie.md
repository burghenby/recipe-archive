# Tropical Smoothie

**Source**: *Produce for Kids* recipe card available at Meijer grocery stores in 2018
---
**Makes**: 2 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 c. Frozen Peaches
- 1 tsp. Vanilla Extract
- 1 c. 1% Milk
- 1/2 Banana
- 2 T. Honey
- Ice (if desired)

## Directions

Place peaches, vanilla, milk, banana and honey in blender and blend until smooth. Add ice as desired for thicker smoothie.
