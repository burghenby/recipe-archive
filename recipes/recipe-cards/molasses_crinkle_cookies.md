# Molasses Spice Crinkles

**Source**: *Betty Crocker* grocery store recipe card, circe 2013
---

## Ingredients

- 2 c. Gold Medal all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp. Baking Powder
- 1 tsp. Baking Soda
- 1 tsp. Ground Ginger
- 1 tsp. Ground Cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp. Ground Nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp. Salt
- 1/4 tsp. Ground Gloves
- 1/4 tsp. Ground Allspice
- 3/4 c. Shortening
- 1 c. Granulated Sugar
- 1 Egg
- 1/4 c. Molasses
- 1 c. Coarse White Sparkling Sugar

## Directions

1. In medium bow, mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, cloves and allspice; set aside.
2. In large bowl, beat shortening with electric mixer on medium speed until fluffy. Gradually add granulated sugar, beating well. Add egg and molasses; beat well. Gradually add flour mixture, beating on low speed until blended. Cover; refrigerate 1 hour.
3. Heat oven to 375°F. Shape dough to 1-inch balls; roll in sparkling sugar. On ungreased cookie sheet, place balls 2 inches apart.
4. Bake 9 to 11 minutes (tops will crack) or just until set. Remove from cookie sheet to cooling racks.
