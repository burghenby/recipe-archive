# Balsamic Chicken and Peppers

- **Source**: *Taste of Home* magazine recipe card insert by Valerie Moore
---
**Total Time**: 25 minutes\
**Makes**: 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (4oz.each)
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- 1/8 tsp. salt
- 2 Tbsp. olive oil, divided
- 1 each medium sweet yellow, red and orange peppers, julienned
- 1 small onion, sliced
- 2 Tbsp. balsamic vinegar

1. Sprinkle chicken with pepper and salt. In a large skillet, cook chicken in 1 Tbsp. oil for 4-6 minutes one each side or until a thermometer reads 170Â°; drain. Remove chicken and keep warm.
2. In the same skillet, saute peppers and onion in remaining oil for 3-4 minutes or until tender. Return chicken to the pan. Add vinegar; heat through.
