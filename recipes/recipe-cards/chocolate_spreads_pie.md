# Chocolate Spreads Pie

**Source**: *Hershey's* Kitchens recipe card
---
**Makes**: 8 servings

## Ingredients

- One 9-inch baked and cooled pie crust
- 1-1/4 c. (13-oz. jar) HERSHEY'S Spreads Chocolate, HERSHEY'S Spreads Caramel flavored Chocolate or REESE'S Spreads Peanut Butter Chocolate, divided
- 4 oz. Cream Cheese, softened
- 1/2 c. Milk
- 1/3 c. Powdered Sugar
- 1 pkg. (4-serving size) Instant Vanilla Pudding and Pie Filling (NOTE: Get actual typical pkg. size of instant pudding)
- 1 c. (1/2 pt.) Heavy Whipping Cream
- Sweetened Whipped Cream (optional)

## Directions

1. Prepare pie crust; cool.
2. Beat 3/4 cup chocolate spread or peanut butter chocolate spread and cream cheese in medium bowl until smooth./
Add milk, powdered sugar and instant pudding mix; beat until smooth. Set aside.
3. In separate bowl, beat whipping cream until stiff; fold into chocolate mixture. Spread in baked crust.
4. Place remaining 1/2 cup spread in microwave-safe bowl Microwave at MEDIUM (50%) 30 seconds; stir./
If necessary, microwave at MEDIUM an additional 10 seconds at a tie, stirring after each heating, until spread is fluid and smooth when stirred. Carefully spread over chocolate mixture in crust. Cover; refrigerate several hours or overnight until firm.
5. Garnish with sweetened whipped cream, if desired. Cover; refrigerate leftovers.
