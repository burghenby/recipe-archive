# Chunky Chocolate Cookies

**Source**: *Pillsbury* grocery store recipe card, circa 2012
---

## Ingredients

- 1 roll (16.5 oz.) Pillsbury refrigerated sugar cookies
- 1 c. Semisweet Chocolate Chunks
- 1/2 c. Miniature Semisweet Chocolate Chips
- 1/2 c. Chopped Pecans
- 3 T. Unsweetened Baking Cocoa

## Directions

1. Heat oven to 350°F. In a large bowl, break up cookie dough. Add remaining ingredients; mix well.
2. Onto ungreased cookie sheets, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart. Bake 8 to 11 minutes or until set. Cool 2 minutes; remove from cookie sheets to cooling racks.

## Variation Tip

Substitute miniature candy-coated chocolate candies for the miniature chips and another favorite nut for the pecans.
