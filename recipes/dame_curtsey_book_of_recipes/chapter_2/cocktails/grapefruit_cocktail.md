## Directions - Original

For six persons, mix eight tablespoonfuls of grapefruit juice with two of sugar syrup, add two tablespoonfuls of maraschino if liked. Add a few tiny pieces of the pulp and pour the whole into small glasses filled with cracked ice. This should be drunk from the glass and not eaten with the spoon.
