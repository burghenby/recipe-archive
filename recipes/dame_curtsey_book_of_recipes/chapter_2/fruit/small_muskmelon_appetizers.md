## Directions - Original

Cut the shell into a basket, with part of the rind left as a handle Scoop out the pulp, breaking it as little as possible. Add to the meat from four small melons, four tablespoonfuls of sherry, two sliced bananas, one cupful of red raspberries, and a quarter of a cupful of powdered sugar. Heap this mixture in the melon basket and serve ice-cold.
