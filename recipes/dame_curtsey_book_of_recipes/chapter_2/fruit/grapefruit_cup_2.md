## Directions - Original

Cut fine grapefruit exactly in halves, scooping out all the pulp, and with sharp scissors cutting the edges of the rinds into points or scallops. Sweeten the pulp, add shredded pine apple. Halve white grapes, and cut preserved ginger into thin slices, fill the rinds and dot maraschino cherries over the top. Pour a little grapefruit juice over all. Keep on ice till time to serve.
