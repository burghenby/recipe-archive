# Anchovy Canapes

- **Source**: *"Dame Curtsey's" Book of Recipes*
---

## Ingredients

- White bread, cut in oblong strips
- Butter
- Mayonnaise dressing - See issue #29
- Anchovies, boneless
- Eggs, hardboiled
- Beets, minced

## Directions - Updated

1. Divide the hardboiled eggs, keeping the whites and yolks separate before mincing them.

## Directions - Original

Cut white bread in oblong strips, toast and spread lightly with butter and mayonnaise dressing. Lay two or three strips of boneless anchovies across toast diagonally and fill in spaces with minced hard-boiled eggs and minced beets; first the white, then the beet, and lastly the yolk. A pretty and delicious appetizer.
