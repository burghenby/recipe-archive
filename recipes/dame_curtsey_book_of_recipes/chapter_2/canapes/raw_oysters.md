## Directions - Original

Allow six oysters to each person. Buy in the shell, Blue Points are the best. Keep on ice, on the lower half shell; place on oyster plates with finely cracked ice. Put a half lemon cut in quarters in the centre of the plate. Salt, pepper, cayenne, horseradish, tabasco sauce are the condiments used.
