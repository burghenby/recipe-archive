## Ingredients

- 1/4 box Gelatin
- 1 c Cold chicken loquor
- 3 c Hot chicken stock, seasoned
- 2 c Whipped cream
- Bouillon cups

## Directions - Updated



## Directions - Original

Soak one-fourth box of gelatine in one cup of cold chicken liquor; when softened add three cups of hot chicken stock seasoned with herbs, salt, and vegetables; cool and add two cups of whipped cream; turn into a freezer and proceed as for ice-cream; then serve in bouillon cups with saltines.
