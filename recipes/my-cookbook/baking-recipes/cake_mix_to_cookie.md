# Cookies from Cake Mix

**Source**: I'd seen the boxed cake mix method around many places; I don't believe I took this information from any one blog, video, or post. Converting a cake recipe, on the other hand, I have no recollection at all where I got the info I used and if memory serves, it was a miracle that the conversion even worked.

## Ingredients

- Cake Recipe or boxed cake mix
- 2 Eggs
- 1/2 c. Oil

## Directions

2. BOXED CAKE MIX: To the boxed cake mix, add 2 eggs and 1/2 c. oil. Adjust the amount of eggs and oil as necessary per size of cake mix in order to achieve a cookie dough that can be rolled into 1-inch balls.
3. Place rolled cookie dough balls on parchment-lined baking sheets. Bake for 8-12 minutes. Allow to cool on baking sheet for 2 minutes before moving to a wire rack to cool completely.
