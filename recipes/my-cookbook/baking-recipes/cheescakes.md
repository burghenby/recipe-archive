# Cheesecakes
## Basic Recipe
## Ingredients:
- 4-8 oz. pkg. cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp. vanilla extract
- 4 eggs

## Directions:
_Missing bake time_
Preheat oven to 325F. Heat a water bath to 175F.\
Min a large bowl, mix sugar, vanilla, and cream cheese until smooth. Add eggs until blended. Do not over mix.\
Add to pan over choice of crust. Insert pan into water bath. Bake for ??.\
Remove path from both oven and water bath and allow to cool to room temperature. Chill in refrigerator at least 4 hours.\

## Alternatives:
*Each alternative is independent and not intended to be added to one another.*
**Denser and Thicker**: Use only 2 eggs

**Lighter and Creamier**: Use 6 eggs

**Lighter and Creamier with a Slightly Tart Flavor**:
Add 1 cup sour cream

**Lighter and Creamier with a Milky Taste**:
Add 3/4 cup heavy cream

**Thicker, Cake-Like**:
Add 1 Tbsp. flour
(_May carry some flour taste_)

**Thicker, Cake-Like**:
Add 2 Tbsp. cornstarch
