# Blueberry Coffee Cake

**Source**: Via Pinterest, original from **[Spaceships and Laserbeams](https://spaceshipsandlaserbeams.com/blueberry-coffee-cake/)**, circa 2019; I've since made my own adjustments, plus the site shows that the original recipe was last updated March 27th, 2025
---

## Ingredients

- 1/4 c. Butter
- 3/4 c. Sugar + 2 T. set aside
- 1 Egg
- 1 tsp. Vanilla
- 1-1/2 c. Blueberries
- 1-1/2 c. Flour + 1 T. set aside
- 3 tsp. Baking powder
- 1/2 c. Milk
- 3 T. Brown sugar
- 1 tsp. Cinnamon
- 1 tsp. Ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp. Ground nutmeg
- 1/4 tsp. Ground clove
- 1/4 tsp. Allspice

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Cream butter with 3/4 c. sugar. Add egg then vanilla, mixing well after each addition.
2. Add flour and baking powder, alternating with milk. Spread half of mixture a in greased 8x8-in. pan.
3. Combine blueberries with 2 T. sugar and 1 T. flour. Spread evenly over batter in pan.
4. Carefully spread remaining batter over blueberries. In a small bowl, combine brown sugar, cinnamon, ginger, clove, allspice and nutmeg. Sprinkle brown sugar mixture over top.
5. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until wooden skewer inserted into center comes out clean. Take care as pierced or burst blueberries can give a false indication of underdone batter.
