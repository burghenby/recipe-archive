# Chunky Chocolate Cookies

- **Source**: Original recipe was from a Pillsbury grocery store recipe card from 2012 and called for a refrigerated, premade sugar cookie mix. I've adapted my version to use a homemade sugar cookie base.
---

## Ingredients

- 2 c. Flour
- 1/2 tsp. Baking soda
- 1/4 tsp. Salt
- 1-1/4 c. Brown sugar, firmly packed
- 1/2 c. Shortening
- 1/2 c. Butter
- 1 tsp. Vanilla
- 1 Large egg
- 1 c. Semi-sweet chocolate chunks
- 1/2 c. Mini chocolate chips
- 3 T. Cocoa powder (baking)

## Directions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Beat egg, brown sugar, butter, shortening, and vanilla in a large bowl until creamy. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add to creamy mixture, beating on low until just blended. Stir in chocolate chunks and chips until evenly distributed.\
\
Shape dough into 1-in. balls. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet, evenly spaced apart. Bake 12-14 minutes or until centers are just cooked. Cool on pan for 2 min. before moving to wire rack to cool completely.

## Substitute

The purpose of the chunks and mini chips is for a variation of texture, but you can also use your choice of chocolate chips/chunks/morsels up to a total of 1-1/2 cups.

## Alternate

This recipe can be used to make butterscotch chip, white chocolate chip, or other morsel/chip/chunk variation cookies. Simply omit the cocoa powder (if desired) and substitute the chocolate chunks/morsels with your choice of chips/morsels/chunks.
