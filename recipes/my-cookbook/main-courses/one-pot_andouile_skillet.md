# One-Pot Andouille Sausage Skillet Pasta

**Source**: Unsure, believe it was a recipe card from a grocery aisle dispenser
---

## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp. Olive Oil
- 1 Onion, diced
- 2 cups Chicken Broth
- 1/2 cup Milk
- 1 cup Shredded Pepper Jack Cheese
- 2 Cloves Garlic, minced
- 1 pkg. Andouille Sausage, thinnly sliced
- 1-14 oz. can Diced Tomatoes
- 8 oz. Elbow Macaroni
- Kosher Salt, Ground Pepper

## Directions

Heat olive oil in large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic, onion and sausage. Cook, stirring frequently, until sausage is slightly browned. (About 3-4 minutes.)\
\
Stir in chicken broth, tomatoes, milk, and pasta. Season to taste. Bring to boil; Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until pasta is cooked.\
\
Remove from heat and top with cheese. Cover until cheese has melted. Serve immediately.
