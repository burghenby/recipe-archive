# Simple Southern Mac & Cheese

**Source**: Unsure, possible a magazine before or around 2019
---

## Ingredients

- 12 oz. Elbow Macaroni
- 1 can Evaporated Milk
- 3 Tbsp. Butter
- 8 oz. Sharp Cheddar, shredded
- 1 lb. Extra Sharp Cheddar, shredded
- 8 oz. Monterey Jack Cheese, shredded
- Salt and Pepper, to taste
- Herbs, to taste

## Directions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F.\
\
Cook macaroni until tender. Drain, then return to pot with heat on LOW.\
\
Combine all ingredients in pot. Stir until melted together, then pour into 9 X 13-inch casserole dish.\
\
Bake for 30 minutes.\
