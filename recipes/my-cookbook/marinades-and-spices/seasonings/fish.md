# Fish Seasoning
---
## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp. dry basil
- 1 Tbsp. crushed rosemary
- 1 Tbsp. dry parsley
- 2 tsp. salt
- 2 tsp. ground black pepper
- 2 tsp. dry thyme
- 2 tsp. dry marjoram
- 1 tsp. dry oregano
- 1 tsp celary salt
- 1 tsp garlic powder

## Uses

A general-purpose fish seasoning, very good for oven-baked fish.
