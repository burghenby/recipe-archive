# Homemade Alfredo Sauce
---

## Ingredients

- 4 Tbsp. Butter
- 1 cup Heavy Cream
- 2 cloves Garlic, minced
- 1-1/2 cups Parmesan Cheese, freshly grated

## Directions

Melt butter in saucepan over medium-low heat. Add heavy cream and allow to simmer for 5-7 minutes. Add garlic and cheese and whisk continuously until the cheese is completely melted.
