# Hummus

For all combinations, combine ingredients in a food processor or a bowl with a stick blender and blend until smooth.

## Classic
- 1 can Chickpeas
- 1/3 cup Tahini
- 2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
- 2 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
- 1 clove Garlic

## Southwest
- 1 can Black Beans
- 1 Chipotle Pepper
- 2 Tbsp. Lime Juice
- 1/4 cup Cilantro Leaves
- 1 tsp. Cumin

## Pesto
- 1 can Chickpeas
- 1/3 cup Tahini
- 2 Tbsp. Pesto
- 2 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
- 1 Tbsp. Grated Parmesan Cheese

## Greek Herb
- 1 can Chickpeas
- 1/2 cup Basil Leaves
- 1/2 cup Parsley Leaves
- 1/4 cup Tarragon Leaves
- 2 Tbsp. Olive Oil

## Italian
- 1 can Chickpeas
- 1/4 cup Subdried Tomatoes
- 2 Tbsp. Olive Oil
- 2 Tbsp. Lemon Juice
- 1 tsp. Dried Oregano

## Ranch
- 1 can Chickpeas
- 1/3 cup Greek Yogurt, plain
- 1 tsp. Dried Parsley
- 1/2 tsp. Garlic Salt
- 1 tsp. Dried Dill
