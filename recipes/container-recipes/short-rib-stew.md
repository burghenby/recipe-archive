# Short Rib Stew

**Source**: *Tiborang* Grace 7-in-1 Multifunction Cooker User Guide
---

## Ingredients

- 3-1/2 lb. boneless beef short ribs, cut into 1-1/2-in. pieces
- 1/2 c. all-purpose flour
- 3 T. extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 c. dry red wine
- 3 c. veal or chicken stock
- 6 carrots, cut into 1/2-in. dice
- 1-1/2 lb. Yukon gold potatoes, peels and cut into 1/2-in. dice
- 3 medium parsnips, peeled and cut into 1/2-in. dice
- 1 large onion, cut into 1-in. dice
- 1/2 lb. cremini mushrooms, quartered
- 1 T. thyme leaves, chopped
- 8 sage leaves, coarsely chopped
- Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper

## Directions

1. In a large bowl, toss the short ribs with the flour. Use medium heat on the Tiborang frying pan and apply oil. Working in batches, cook the short ribs over moderately medium heat until browned all over, about 6-10 minutes per batch. Discard the oil.
2. Return the meat and any juices to the wok. Add wine and boil until reduced by half. Add the stock and bring to a simmer. Cover and cook over low heat until the mat is tender, estimate 2 hours.
3. Skim any fat from the stew. Stir in the carrots, potatoes, parsnips, onion, mushrooms, thyme and sage. Cover and simmer over lowheat until the vegetables are tender, about 35 minutes. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
