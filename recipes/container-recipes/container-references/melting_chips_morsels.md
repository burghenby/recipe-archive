# Melting Chocolate Morsels/Chips

- **Source**: *Nestle Toll House* White & Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsel chip bags
---
## Microwave

- Microwave 1 cup morsels in uncovered, microwave-safe bowl for 45 seconds; Stir.
- If needed, heat at additional 15 second intervals, stirring vigorously after each interval until melted.
- Microwaves vary, melting times may need to be adjusted.

## Double Boiler

- Place 1-2 cups morsels in top of double boiler over hot (not boiling) water. Do not cover. Prevent water from coming in contact with morsels.
- When morsels begins to turn shiny, stir constantly until melted. Once melted, immediately remove top pan with melted morsels from bottom pan.
- We do not recommend melting more than 2 cups of morsels at a time. Once melted, use immediately.

## To Store Coconut
Store in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate after opening.
