# Sweetened Coconut Flakes

- **Source**: *Giant Eagle* sweetened coconut flakes bag
---
## To Toast Coconut

Preheat oven to 325°F. Place a thin layer of coconut flakes on a baking sheet.\
Bake for 5-10 minutes, until golden brown. After a couple of minutes, stir to ensure even toasting.

## To Tint Coconut

Place 1 cup of coconut flakes in a screwtop jar. Combine 1 to 2 drops of food coloring in 1 teaspoon of water; add to coconut.\
Screw lid and shake well until coconut is uniformly colored.

## To Store Coconut
Store in a cool, dry place. Refrigerate after opening.
