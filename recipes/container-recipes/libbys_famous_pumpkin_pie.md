# Libby's Famous Pumpkin Pie

- **Source**: *Libby's* 100% Pure Pumpkin can
---

## Ingredients

- 1 can (29 oz) Libby's 100% Pure Pumpkin
- 3 c. (24 fl. oz.) Carnation Evaporated Milk
- 1-1/2 c. granulated sugar
- 4 large eggs
- 1 tsp. salt
- 2 tsp. ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp. ground ginger
- 1/2 tsp. ground cloves
- 2 unbaked 9-in. (4-c. volume) deep-dish pie shells
- Whipped cream (optional)

## Directions

- Mix dry ingredients in medium bowl. Beat eggs in large bowl. Stir in pumpkin and dry ingredient mixture. Gradually stir in milk.
- Pour into pie shells.
- Bake in preheated 425°F oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350°F; bake for 30-40 minutes or until pie edges are firm and center is set. 
- Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate. Top with whipped cream before serving
