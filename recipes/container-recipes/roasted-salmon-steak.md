# Roasted Salmon Steak

**Source**: *Tiborang* Grace 7-in-1 Multifunction Cooker User Guide
---
**Prep Time**: (Omit if not provided by recipe)
**Total Time**: (Omit if not provided by recipe)
**Makes**: (Omit if not provided by recipe)

## Ingredients

- 4 (12-oz.) salmon steaks, bones removed
- Kosher salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 2 T. extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 T. butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 T. honey
- 1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
- Freshly chopped parsley, for serving

## Directions

1. Season salmon on both sides with salt and pepper. Be cautious to only use medium heat in the Tiborang frying pan, after apply heat oil and butter, add salmon and cook until bottom is golden, 5 minutes, then flip. Add garlic, lemon juice, honey, and red pepper flakes.
2. Cook until salmon is cooked through, another 5 minutes, spooning sauce over salmon as it cooks.
3. Garnish with parsley before serving.
