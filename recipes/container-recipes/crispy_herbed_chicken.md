# Crispy Herbed Chicken

- **Source**: *Quaker* yellow Corn Meal container
---
**Makes**: 5 Servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c. QUAKER or PEARL MILLING COMPANY Enriched COrn MEal
- 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
- 2 T. grated parmesan cheese
- 1-1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
- 1/4 tsp. garlic salt (optional)
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- One 2-1/2 to 3 lb. broiler-fryer chicken, cut up, skinned
- 1/2 c. skim milk
- 3 T. butter, melted

## Directions

Heat oven to 400°F. Combine dry ingredients. Dip chicken into milk; coat with corn meal mixture.\
Place chicken on rack in 15x10-inch pan; brush with butter. Bake 40 to 45 minutes or until golden brown and tender.
