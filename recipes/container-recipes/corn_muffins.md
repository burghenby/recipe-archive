# Corn Muffins

- **Source**: *Quaker* yellow Corn Meal container (A variation from the Easy Corn Bread)
---
**Makes**: 12 muffins

## Ingredients

- 1-1/4 c. all-purpose flour
- 3/4 c. QUAKER or PEARL MILLING COMPANY Enriched COrn MEal
- 1/4 c. sugar
- 2 tsp. baking powder
- 1/2 tsp. salt (optional)
- 1 c. skim milk
- 1/4 c. vegetable oil
- 2 egg whites or 1 egg, beaten

## Directions

Heat oven to 400°F. Combine dry ingredients. Stir in milk, oil and egg, mixing just until dry ingredients are moistened.\
Pour batter into 12 greased or paper-lined medium muffin cups. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until light golden brown.
