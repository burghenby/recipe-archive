# Favorite Hot Cocoa

- **Source**: *Hershey's* Cocoa Tub
---
**Prep Time**: (Omit if not provided by recipe)
**Total Time**: (Omit if not provided by recipe)
**Makes**: 6 Servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup hot water
- 1/4 cup Hershey's Cocoa
- 4 cups milk
- 3/4 tsp. vanilla extract
- Dash of salt

## Directions

1. Mix sugar, cocoa, and salt in saucepan; stir in water.
2. Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture boils; boil and stir 2 minutes.
3. Stir in milk and heat. DO NOT BOIL.
4. Remove from heat, add vanilla.
