# Meringue

- **Source**: *Giant Eagle* sweetened coconut flakes bag
---
## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup flour
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 2 cups milk
- 3 egg yolks, beaten
- 2 Tbsp. butter
- 1 tsp. vanilla extract
- 1 cup Giant Eagle sweetened coconut flakes
- 8-inch pie shell, baked

## Directions

Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Combine sugar, flour, and salt in a heavy saucepan. Stir in milk. Heat on stovetop over medium heat, until mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Remove from heat and add egg yolks; return to heat and cook 2 minutes. Remove from heat and add butter, vanilla, and coconut. Mix well and pour into baked pie shell. Cover with meringue. Bake until meringue is golden brown, about 12-15 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
