# Simple Sugar Cookies

**Source**: *Baker's Corner (Aldi's)* Granulated Sugar
---

## Ingredients

- 1 c. Granulated SUgar
- 1 c. Butter or margarine, softened
- 1 Egg
- 1 T. Vanilla extract
- 2-1/4 c. All-purpose flour
- 1 tsp. Baking soda

## Directions

In a large bowl, mix sugar and butter. Beat in egg and vanilla until light and fluffy. Mix in flour and baking soda. 
Divide dough into halves. Shape each half into a roll about 1-1/2 in. in diameter. Wrap each half in wax paper and refrigerate for approximately 1 hour until chilled. 
Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Unwrap rolls and cut into 1/4-in. slices. Sprinkle each slice with a pinch of sugar. Place on ungreased pan and bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned around the edges. 
Cool on wire rack. Store in an airtight container once the cookies have cooled.
