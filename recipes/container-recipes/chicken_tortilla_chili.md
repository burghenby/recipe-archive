# Chicken Tortilla Chili

- **Source**: *Aldi's Chef's Cupboard* Reduced Sodium Chicken Broth carton
- Courtney of Aldi Test Kitchen
---
**Prep Time**: 5 minutes\
**Total Time**: 45 minutes\
**Makes**: (Omit if not provided by recipe)

## Ingredients

- 5 c. Chef's Cupboard Reduced Sodium Chicken Broth, divided
- 2 15.5-oz. cans cannellini beans, drained and rinsed, divided
- 1-1/2 lb. chicken breasts
- 1 tsp. bround cumin
- 1 tsp. paprika
- 1 tsp. chili powder
- 2 limes, juiced
- 1/2 tsp. cayenne pepper
- 3 T. pure olive oil, divided
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 jalapeno, ribs and seeds removed, minced
- 15-1/2 oz. kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 15-1/2 oz. black beans, drained and rinsed
- 16 oz. organic medium salsa
- 1 c. crushed restraurant style tortilla chips
- ground sea salt, to taste
- 

## Directions

1. In a blender or food processor, combine 2 cups chicken broth with one can of cannellini beans. Blend until smooth. Set aside.
2. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. In a medium bowl, toss together chicken, cumin, paprika, chili powder, lime juice, cayenne and 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
3. In a large pot,  over medium heat, heat remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil. Add onion, garlic and jalapeno. Saute for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add chicen and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Add reserved chicken broth mixture, remaining 3 cups chicken broth, beans, salsa and tortilla chips. Season to taste with salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat to a simmer, continue cooking for 30 minutes.
5. Serve with fresh cilantro, sliced green onions, sour cream and shredded cheese.

## Tip

If you don't have a food processor or blender, you can achieve the same thickening results by smashing beans by hand.
