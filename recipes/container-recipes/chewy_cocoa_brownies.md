# Chewy Cocoa Brownies

- **Source**: *Nestle Toll House* Baking Cocoa Tub
---
**Makes**: 24 Bars

## Ingredients

- 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 cup Nestle Toll House Baking Cocoa
- 1/2 tsp. baking powder
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1 2/3 cups granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup (1 1/2 sticks) butter or margarine, melted
- 2 Tbsp. water
- 2 large eggs
- Powdered sugar (optional)

## Directions

PREHEAT oven to 350Â°F. Grease 13 x 9-inch baking pan.

COMBINE flour, cocoa, baking powder and salt in medium bowl. COmbine granulated sugar, butter and water in large bowl. Stir in eggs and vanilla extract. Stir in flour mixture. Stir in nuts. Spread into prepared baking pan.

BAKE for 18 to 25 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out slightly sticky. Cool completely on wire rack. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. Cut into bars.
