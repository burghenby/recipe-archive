# Crispy Herbed Fish

- **Source**: *Quaker* yellow Corn Meal container
---
**Makes**: 6 Servings

## Ingredients

- 1/2 c. QUAKER or PEARL MILLING COMPANY Enriched COrn MEal
- 1/4 c. all-purpose flour
- 2 T. grated parmesan cheese
- 1-1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
- 1/4 tsp. garlic salt (optional)
- 1/4 tsp. pepper
- One 1-1/2 lb. fresh or frozen, thawed perch or cod fillets
- 1/2 c. skim milk

## Directions

Heat oven to 400°F. Combine dry ingredients. Dip fish into milk; coat with corn meal mixture.\
Place fish skin side down on rack in broiler pan. Broil 5 inches from heat 5 to 8 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork and turns golden brown.
